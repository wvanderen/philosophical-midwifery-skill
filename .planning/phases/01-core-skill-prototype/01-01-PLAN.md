## Phase Goal

**As a** user bringing a felt disturbance to a compatible chat/coding agent,
**I want to** load a single skill package that reliably leads me through disciplined Socratic examination of the belief beneath my disturbance,
**so that** I leave with an examined proposition (and the agent never drifts into therapy, advice, diagnosis, or essays — and stops cleanly if a crisis signal appears).

Source: ROADMAP.md Phase 1 `**Goal:**` line, restated in user-story form per MVP_MODE.

## Objective

Author the complete v1 `philosophical-midwifery` Agent Skill package — four pure-markdown files authored to the 16 locked CONTEXT.md decisions (D-01..D-16), grounded by the copy-ready templates in `01-RESEARCH.md`, and threaded by the single shared "recognition determines worth" worked example (D-12).

**Purpose:** This is the entire Phase 1 deliverable. The skill must be loadable, runnable, and safe out of the box — there is no Phase 1 work outside these four files. Authoring them as one plan (rather than fragmenting across waves) preserves cross-file consistency on the shared example, on the D-08 hypothesis protocol (echoed in SKILL.md and PATHOLOGOS_PATTERNS.md), and on the D-16 placement rule (modulation in SKILL.md; hard stops in SAFETY_BOUNDARIES.md).

**Output:** Four new files written under `philosophical-midwifery/`. No code, no schema, no templates, no validator — all of those are locked Out-of-Scope (Phase 2/3).
